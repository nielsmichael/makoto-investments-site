What is "Stand By You"?

"Stand By You" is an employee-centered consulting service designed to prevent business and employees from workplace-related mental health crises and accidents.
Our process uses an "early detection, early prevention" approach to ensure that both your business and your employees are protected.

Don't wait to feel the impact of work-related employee stress.

Prevent it.

Stand By You services include:

  • 1-on-1 employee consultation
  • Identify issues with a "Quantify-by-diagnosis" approach
  • Develop customized improvement plans for workers' mental health
  • Produce solutions that lead to mental health improvement
  • Workplace mental health related problems will be identified and treated early on

Lean Consultation-based Analysis

Based on one on one consultation with employees, we will do a deep analysis of the voices obtained during consultations and propose what can be done to improve the working conditions using our lean methodology.

We have several workshops available to alleviate stress in the work place:

  • Communication
  • Mental training incl. self-awareness, stress coping, anger management, adaptive ways of thinking…etc.

We can design and conduct a bespoke employee survey to assess stress or mental state and report the analysis as well as proposal of what can be done.

Improving Employee Performance

In All Areas

  1. Organization Goals - Do employees feel the company’s mission or goals make sense to them?
  1. Performance Reviews & Feedback - Is there a system in place to discuss employee performance? Is that system having a meaningful impact on the wokrplace?
  1. Job Fit - Is the content of the job and required skills clear?
    Does the employee meet these requirements?
  1. Coaching & Training - Is there a training or coaching system in place? Is that system focused on improving the employee performance and motivation? Does the training / coaching focused on improving and validating the needs and skillsets of the workers?
  1. Assessment - Are there tools in place for a comprehensive assessment of employees' skills, characteristics, personality traits, personal goals (ex: wanting to work at an overseas branch) and individual conditions (ex: family circumstances)?
  1. Continuous Improvement - Does your business have a culture of continuous improvement for both the organization - and it's employees? Are there checks in place to ensure that this "continuous improvement" actually continues?
  1. Person-Organization Fit - Is the work environment appropriate? Are the employees' personalities / characteristics compatible with the organization and other workers?
    (Ex: employees who value communication w/ organizations that value individual work)
In addition to reducing risk of mental health related accidents and lowering your turnover rate, the Stand By You service also directly impacts and improves employee performance in these areas.
