What is TWI?

"Training Within Industry (TWI) is a dynamic and proven method of hands-on training, learning and coaching for supervisors, team leaders, and workers." -TWI Institute

Makoto Global is the exclusive affiliate of the TWI Institute in Japan, and our CEO Brad Schmidt sits on the TWI Institute board.

TWI is a dynamic program of hands-on learning and practice, teaching essential skills for supervisors, team leaders, and anyone who directs the work of others. It is an essential element of Lean programs & systems utilized around the world - such as in the Toyota Production System (TPS).

The program was born out of the need to rapidly train unskilled workers entering the production workforce, and was introduced in Japan during post-war rebuilding. It is still in widespread use across the country.

TWI Course Programs

From the TWI Institute

Job Instruction (JI)

Develops process stability by developing the "one best way" to do a job. Creates a solid training foundation for improvement programs.

Job Relations (JR)

Teaches supervisors how to handle relationship issues with a people-centric view and prevent them from happening.

Job Methods (JM)

Encourages higher productivity and output by improving the way jobs get done and eliminating, combining, rearranging and simplifying steps in the process.

TWI Problem Solving (PS)

Consolidates and integrates the proven TWI programs of JI, JR, and JM, enabling frontline supervisors and workers to be self-sufficient and solve the problems they face more quickly and collaboratively.
